About EPR2 Musix

EPR2 Musix, whose real name is Emman Elumir, born in April 2006, is an independent music artist from the Philippines. Known for his experimental and electronic sound, he has collaborated with artists such as Luice, EmzzBruhh, HIZURE, and 5INE. Active since 2021, his music is available on Spotify, Amazon Music, and YouTube. EPR2 Musix maintains a mysterious persona while continuing to build a loyal fanbase with each release.
